# Capital framing

A conservative horizon loss proxy at horizon \(H\) for the protection seller is:

$$
L = (1-R)\frac{N}{d}\, DF(\tau_{(k)})\,\mathbf{1}\{\tau_{(k)}\le H\}.
$$

## Metrics

- \(EL=\mathbb{E}[L]\)
- \(VaR_\alpha\) is the \(\alpha\)-quantile
- \(ES_\alpha = \mathbb{E}[L \mid L \ge VaR_\alpha]\)
